SPT

The Schtewit Poker Tour league was founded in 2006 by a group of friends who needed an excuse to get away from their wives and stay in contact with friends.

A SPT season consists of 12 nights; 2 games per night at $60 per game; $10 of which is placed into a prize pool and runs every 2 weeks. Points are awarded to members that finish in first, second, third and forth for each game. 5 points awarded for first, 3 points for second, 2 points for third and 1 point for forth. The top 8 players with the most points during the season, play the final table game for the prize pool of money accumulated throughout the season.

Non SPT members are allowed to play at a cost of $50 per game and are NOT allowed to play for the final prize pool of money.
